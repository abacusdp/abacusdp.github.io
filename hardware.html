CleverTags

> The Personnel, Patient and Asset Tracking R.T.L.S comprises of signal emitting active BLE tags which would be attached to the current ID cards for personnel,thermometer enabled wristbands for patients and tags attached with industrial grade adhesives to the movable equipment.
> These tags have an accelerometer for capturing steps taken and generating static tag alerts, powered by replaceable coin cell battery which are designed and assembled in India.



Reader

>The hospital premises are demarcated into zones, every zone has corresponding BLE reader devices which would detect the presence of BLE Tags.
> These devices would then communicate the presence data (Tag id, Timestamp, Zone, Distance from Reader) to the On-premise Server.
> Live location of multiple tags can be monitored across multiple floors/ locations simultaneously.
